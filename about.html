About PixelDrop

What is PixelDrop?

PixelDrop is a free download website for Minecraft Pocket Edition (MCPE) texture packs and addons. All content is carefully curated to ensure the best quality for MCPE players worldwide.

Available Content

PixelDrop offers two main categories:

  • Texture Pack — Transform the visual appearance of your Minecraft world into something more realistic, stylish, or unique.
  • Addon — Add new features, items, mobs, or mechanics to your game without changing core gameplay.

How to Download

Super easy! Find the content you want, click on the thumbnail, read the description, then click the Download button. You'll be redirected to MediaFire to download the file.

100% Free

All content on PixelDrop is completely free. No hidden fees, no account required — just download and enjoy.
